About Trigat Education Centre

Trigat Education Centre is a boutique education hub established in 2000 by Reena Dhiman. Our mission is to provide personalized learning experiences, nurturing creativity, skill, and confidence for every student. We specialize in courses like Fashion Designing, Art & Craft, Beautician, and more.

Our dedicated team of instructors ensures each student receives hands-on guidance and expert mentorship. We focus on building confidence, discipline, and creativity in a supportive environment.
