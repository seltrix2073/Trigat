About Trigat Education Centre

Trigat Education Centre is a boutique education hub established in 2000 by Reena Dhiman. Our mission is to provide personalized learning experiences, nurturing creativity, skill, and confidence for every student.

We specialize in Fashion Designing, Art & Craft, Beautician, and more, helping students grow professionally in a supportive environment.
